DAY 1

The first day of She Se PUede, I learned about Ohm's law (V=IR), what each letter means, and how to remember what each letter means. V stands for Voltage which is pressure in the pipes. I stands for Current which is the speed of water in the pipes. R stands for Resistance which can be remembered as sponge in the pipes. I learned that a photo resistor is a resistor that changes brightness based on the light waves that hit it. I also learned how to make an LED light at different speeds.

DAY 2

On the second day of She Se Puede, I learned that RGD is red green blue and I used an RGB and an Arduino to program the RGB to change to different color. I also learned hwo to use a sound detector. Additionally, I learned about the different types of circuits. For example, a series circuit, and a parallel ciruit. In a series circuit the electrons only go in one direction and in a parallel circuit the electrons go in 2 or more directions.

DAY 3

On this day of She Se Puede, I learned about Servo Motors and DC Motors. Servo Motors have three wires while DC Motors have two, Servo Motors are controlled to move in angles but DC Motors controlled by turning the on and off. I also learned what an LCD, Liquid Crystal Display, was and how to program it to play the snake game on it. The class also played two rounds of Adlibs and learned about colleges. With the Arduino I played the Mario theme song.
